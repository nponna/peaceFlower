Our classrooms are arranged so that children ages three, four and five are in the same room. This allows the older children to explore at a different level and also serve as role models for the younger ones. Exposing children to different age groups allows for seamless transitions within the groups. We also try to have the same teacher for those three years, which aids in a close teacher-student relationships. The multi-age aspect encourages older children to guide and be role models to the younger children thereby building confidence and strong self-esteem.
